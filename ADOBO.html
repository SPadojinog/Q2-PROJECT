ADOBO RECIPE


  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 (3 pound) chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 large onion, quartered and sliced
  • 2 tablespoons minced garlic
  • ⅔ cup low sodium soy sauce
  • ⅓ cup white vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 2 teaspoons black pepper
  • 1 bay leaf

Instructions:

  1. Marinate the meat: In a bowl, combine the soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, bay leaves, and peppercorns. Add the meat and marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Cook the adobo: Heat oil in a pot over medium heat. Add the marinated meat and cook for about 5 minutes, until browned.
  3. Simmer: Add the marinade (including garlic, bay leaves, and peppercorns), water, and a pinch of salt. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for 30-40 minutes or until the meat is tender and the sauce has thickened.
  4. Serve: Adjust seasoning with salt and pepper, then serve with steamed rice.
