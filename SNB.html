SINIGANG NA BABOY RECIPE


  • 1 lb pork belly or pork ribs (cut into chunks)
  • 1 onion (quartered)
  • 2 tomatoes (quartered)
  • 2-3 long green peppers (siling pang-sigang)
  • 1-2 radishes (sliced)
  • 1 cup string beans (sitao)
  • 1 eggplant (sliced)
  • 6 cups water
  • 1 pack sinigang mix or 1/4 cup fresh tamarind (adjust to taste)
  • Fish sauce or salt (to taste)
  • 1 tbsp sugar (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Boil the pork: In a large pot, combine the pork and water. Bring to a boil and cook for about 45 minutes until the pork is tender.
  2. Add vegetables: Add the onions, tomatoes, radishes, green peppers, eggplant, and string beans. Continue to simmer for 10-15 minutes.
  3. Add the souring agent: Add the souring agent: Stir in the sinigang mix or fresh tamarind. Season with fish sauce, salt, and sugar (if using) to taste.
  4. Serve: Once the vegetables are tender, serve the sinigang hot with steamed rice.
