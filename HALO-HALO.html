HALO-HALO RECIPE


  • Crushed ice
  • 1/4 cup sweetened jackfruit (langka)
  • 1/4 cup sweetened red beans
  • 1/4 cup sweetened coconut strings (macapuno)
  • 1/4 cup nata de coco
  • 1/4 cup leche flan
  • 1 scoop ube ice cream
  • 1/2 cup evaporated milk
  • Sugar (optional, to taste)

Instructions:

  1. Layer the ingredients: In a tall glass or bowl, layer the sweetened jackfruit, red beans, coconut strings, nata de coco, and leche flan.
  2. Add ice: Fill the glass with crushed ice until almost full.
  3. Top with ube ice cream: Place a scoop of ube ice cream on top.
  4. Pour milk: Drizzle evaporated milk over the ice and ingredients. You can add sugar if you'd like it sweeter.
  5. Mix and enjoy: Mix everything together before eating to enjoy the blend of flavors and textures.
