TAHO RECIPE


  • 1/2 cup silken tofu (steamed or warmed)
  • 1/4 cup sweet syrup (made from brown sugar and water)
  • 1/4 cup sago pearls (tapioca pearls, cooked)

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the tofu: Prepare the tofu: Steam or warm the silken tofu until soft and creamy.
  2. Assemble the taho: In a glass or cup, layer the warm tofu, then add a spoonful of cooked sago pearls.
  3. Add syrup: Drizzle sweet syrup over the tofu and sago pearls.
  4. Pour milk: Drizzle evaporated milk over the ice and ingredients. You can add sugar if you'd like it sweeter.
  5. Serve: Optionally, add ice cubes for a cooler treat, then mix everything together before enjoying!
