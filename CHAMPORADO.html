CHAMPORADO RECIPE


  • 1 cup glutinous rice
  • 4 cups water
  • 1/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup milk (optional)
  • Condensed milk (optional, for topping)

Instructions:

  1. Cook the rice:In a pot, combine glutinous rice and water. Bring to a boil, then simmer for 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the rice is tender.
  2. Add cocoa and sugar: Stir in cocoa powder, sugar, and salt. Mix well until smooth and the chocolate is fully dissolved.
  3. Add milk: Add milk for a creamier texture, if desired. Stir and cook for another 2-3 minutes
  4. Serve: Serve hot, topped with condensed milk for extra sweetness.
