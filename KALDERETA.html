KALDERETA RECIPE


  • 1 lb beef stew meat (cut into chunks)
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 2 potatoes (sliced)
  • 1 carrot (sliced)
  • 1/2 cup bell peppers (chopped)
  • 1/4 cup liver spread
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 1/4 cup green olives (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Sear the beef:Sear the beef: Heat oil in a pot and brown the beef chunks. Set aside.
  2. Cook the vegetables: In the same pot, saut√© onion and tomatoes until soft. Add liver spread and mix well.
  3. Simmer the stew:Add the beef back to the pot along with soy sauce, tomato sauce, and beef broth. Simmer for 1-1.5 hours or until beef is tender.
  4. Add vegetables:Add potatoes, carrots, and bell peppers. Cook until vegetables are tender. Season with salt, pepper, and olives (if using).
  5. Serve: Once the stew thickens and all ingredients are tender, serve hot with rice.
