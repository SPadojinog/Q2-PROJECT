LECHE FLAN RECIPE


  • 1 can (14 oz) sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 can (12 oz) evaporated milk
  • 6 large egg yolks
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup sugar (for caramel)

Instructions:

  1. Make the caramel: In a pan, melt sugar over low heat until golden brown. Pour the caramel into the bottom of a llanera or flan mold, tilting to coat the bottom evenly.
  2. Prepare the flan mixture: In a bowl, whisk together the condensed milk, evaporated milk, egg yolks, and vanilla extract until smooth.
  3. Strain the mixture: Pour the mixture through a fine sieve into the caramel-coated mold to remove any lumps.
  4. Steam the flan:Cover the mold with foil. Steam over low heat for about 30-40 minutes, or until the flan is set (test with a toothpick).
  5. Cool and serve: Let it cool, then refrigerate for at least 2 hours. Once chilled, run a knife around the edges and invert onto a plate.
