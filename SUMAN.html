SUMAN RECIPE


  • 2 cups glutinous rice
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • Banana leaves (or parchment paper for wrapping)

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the rice: Prepare the rice: Wash the glutinous rice thoroughly. In a pot, combine rice, coconut milk, sugar, and salt. Cook over low heat, stirring occasionally, until the rice is tender and the liquid is absorbed (about 20-30 minutes).
  2. Wrap the suman: Cut banana leaves into squares. Place a spoonful of rice mixture in the center and fold the sides to form a tight roll or cylinder.
  3. Steam:Arrange the wrapped suman in a steamer. Steam for 30-40 minutes until fully cooked
  4. Serve:Let it cool before serving. Optionally, serve with brown sugar or mango slices on top.
